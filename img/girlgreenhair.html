My Bio

⭐️ https://nadinekovalchuk.art ⭐️


Hello, I’m a Comic Artist and 2D Character Animator from Kharkiv, Ukraine.


Having a Master’s degree in Computer Science and after years of working for an IT company, I decided to go after my childhood dream and to focus on the profession of an artist.


Nowadays, I work as a comic and story artist for various clients. I’m passionate about frame-by-frame animation and projects where I opt to create 2D animations often become my favorite ones. I also have expertise in creating character designs and drawing illustrations for books, posters and other visual materials.


I’m interested in photography, psychology, science and in the world in general.

📩 Want to discuss your project? Feel free to contact me! Or email at business[at]nadinekovalchuk.art

And Have a Nice Day!
